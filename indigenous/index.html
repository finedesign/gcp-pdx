Indigenous History in North America

Warning: Many, if not all, of these resources will contain violence and trauma, as that is the reality of much of U.S. history and current events. Please be aware of this and take care of yourself, particularly if your ancestors are ones who may have lived through these traumatic events and experienced the violence in their bodies.

Also please be aware that the resources here are by no means the extent of the learning that could be done, but hopefully it can give you a place to start in your learning journey.

Book Recommendations

  • The History of White People by Neil Painter Harris
  • Savage Anxieties by Robert A. Williams Jr
  • Utopian legacies by John Mohawk
  • Stand Your Ground by Kelly Brown Douglas
  • 1491: New Revelations of the Americas Before Columbus by Charles C. Mann

Book Recommendations Particularly for Christians

  • Unsettling Truths by Mark Charles and Soong-Chan Rah (Read here: Ch. 1 and Ch. 7)
  • Shalom and the Community of Creation by Randy Woodley
  • Indigenous Theology and the Western Worldview by Randy Woodley
  • Rescuing the Gospel from the Cowboys by Richard Twiss
